## 🔧 Changes & Tweaks

- **New renewable Create recipes** (via KubeJS) — turn common blocks into Create stones and renewable resources. A curated, balanced set: the overpowered options (renewable diamonds, netherite, wither skulls, heart of the sea, etc.) are intentionally left out. Asurine now uses clay, so it's infinitely craftable.
- **Elytra rebalance** — gliding now bleeds horizontal speed quickly, so you can't cruise across the map by hovering. Still great for bailing off an island, crossing a gap, or an emergency descent. Airships and trains stay the real long-distance travel.
- **Default keybinds** — the pack now ships with a sensible keybind layout out of the box.

## 🐛 Fixes

- **Tree Physics** — fixed being able to walk straight through world leaves (a renamed config option had silently re-enabled it). World leaves are solid again; physics still applies to felled trees.

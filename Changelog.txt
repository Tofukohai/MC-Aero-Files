# 🍃 Aero Tofu 0.9.4

A scenic little update — the **End Sea** finally pours into the Overworld (and looks right *with shaders this time*), the rails get grindier, and the menus get a fresh seasonal wardrobe.

## ✨ Added Mods
- **Create: Train Track Rail Grinding** `[1.1.2]` — grind, smooth, and reshape train tracks in place.
- **Create: Hypertube** — re-added on a compatibility build now that the old NeoForge crash is sorted.
- **End Sea Iris Fix** — small in-house mod that makes the End Sea play nice with shaders (details under 🔧 Fixes).

## 🔄 Updated Mods
- **Bits and Balance** → 2.2.10a
- **Create: Big Cannons** → 5.11.5
- **Create: Railways Navigator** → 0.9.1-C6
- **Sky Archipelago** → 1.3.4
- **Tree Physics** → 1.8
- **DragonLib** → 3.0.28
- **Puzzles Lib** → 21.1.51
- **Be Quiet Negotiator** → 2.0.0
- _The whole **Sodium suite** (Sodium, Sodium Extra, Sodium Dynamic Lights, Sodium Options API, Reese's Sodium Options) stays pinned at its current versions for compatibility — intentionally not updated._

## 🌊 New Content
- **The End Sea now floods the Overworld.** A deep, drifting abyss-sea wells up beneath the islands from `y = -64` downward — watch the drag as you sink.
- **The End Sea renders under shaders.** Previously it simply vanished once you turned shaders on; now it's there in all its glow.

## 🎨 Menus & UI
- **Seven new FancyMenu themes:** Cherry Blossom 🌸, Cozy Campfire 🔥, Pumpkin Soup 🎃, Purple Void 🔮, Spooky Season 👻, plus **Light** & **Dark High-Contrast** themes for accessibility.
- Fresh custom **pause-screen layout** and title-screen art.
- A little loading-screen polish on the way in.

## ⚖️ Balance & Cleanup
- **Pulled three stray enchantments** out of enchanting tables, loot, and villager trades: **Chunk Eater**, **Above the Clouds**, and **Aerodynamic**.
- **Sky Archipelago:** trimmed the oversized airship presets and retuned the balanced variant.

## 🔧 Fixes
- **End Sea + shaders:** built a dedicated compatibility mod so the sea is drawn *after* the shader pipeline composites the frame, instead of being erased by it. Works with Photon, Complementary, Solas, and friends — and stays out of the way entirely when shaders are off.
